Somewhere Over The Rainbow

This was Choura’s first line of tablecloths; it is inspired by Judy Garland’s song “Somewhere Over The Rainbow”. The song is about being somewhere special way up high, where no worries exist. Choura connects this feeling to being around the table with family. All her work is hand embroidered in Syria, preserving the traditional way of embroidery while telling modern stories.
