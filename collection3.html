Moorish

Morocco is often described as a country of allure, mystery and beauty and this is certainly in part due to its unique architecture. Moorish architecture is a combination of African and Islamic architecture. The Al-Hambra in Grenada Spain is one of the greatest examples of Moorish architecture. Traits include geometric patterns and Islamic calligraphy. Abstract calligraphy was used on this set to emphasize that. The set is a modern twist on orientalism and Middle Eastern art. It blends in at home, while adding a subtle original touch. The set includes a bag with prayer matt, clothes and Quran.
