Delphi

The “Delphi” prayer set is part of the Grecian collection that was launched in June 2017. The saying “resting on one’s laurel” was the starting point for this collection because it meant that one could relax now because he or she have done something good in the past. The laurel wreath is a Greek headpiece made of interlocking leaves and was the main inspiration for this design. The head piece comes in various sizes but is usually in gold and silver which inspired the colors of the embroidery for this collection. The Delphi prayer set is hand embroidered with metal thread, and includes a bag with a prayer matt, Quran and prayer clothes.
