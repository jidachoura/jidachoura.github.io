About Me

Jida Choura has a BA in illustration and Visual media. She graduated with first class honors from University Of The Arts London (LCC). Though born in the US, she is originally Syrian and her method of working revolves around traditional Syrian embroidery. She has spent her life between Damascus and London and has recently moved to Jeddah She launched ByJida in June 2016, the idea behind the brand is to create modern designs using traditional embroidery. To preserve Syrian handcrafts while creating contemporary pieces. She loves experimenting and uses embroidery to tell stories and share experiences.
