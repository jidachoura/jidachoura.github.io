A Palestinian Mermaid

This project was about modernizing a known fairytale. Jida chose the Little Mermaid as her initial fairy tale; she modernized it by making it a love story between a Palestinian woman and an Israeli man, two people from different worlds.
Original Piece: 6 post cards
