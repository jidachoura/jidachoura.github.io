Argos

Red figure vase painting, which developed in Athens 520 years BC and was the main inspiration when designing the “Argos” prayer set. It is part of the Grecian collection that was launched in June 2017. The hand embroidery here is a traditional Syrian technique done with silk and cane thread, the combination of cutwork with this type of embroidery was used to make the pattern stand out. The Argos prayer set includes a bag with a prayer matt, Quran and prayer clothes all hand embroidered. The set is customizable in color and size
